You can choose different air lines to arrive in China. Specifically, those leading to Nanjing Lukou International Airport are recommended because it will be most convenient for you to get to the conference location from there. Nanjing Lukou International Airport is one of the important transportation hubs in Nanjing and it serves direct flight to and from many Asian as well as European destinations. However, if it’s not so convenient to fly to Nanjing Lukou International Airport from your country, some other destinations in China, such as Beijing Capital International Airport, Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport and Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport are still in your limits of consideration. After arrival, you can transfer to Nanjing Lukou International Airport or even choose a train to get to Nanjing as you wish.

Transportation to Nanjing

Nanjing is a railway hub with two main train stations in the city: Nanjing Railway Station and Nanjing South Railway Station. Meanwhile, Nanjing Lukou International Airport is an important airport, linking this city with many cities home and abroad. Flights to domestic cities such as Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou can be found at your arrival at airport. From abovementioned information, when you arrive in China, you can arrive in Nanjing through following ways.

By Plane (Nanjing Lukou International Airport).

By high-speed rail(Nanjing South Railway Station).

By Train(Nanjing Railway Station).

Transportation to conference location

When you arrive in Nanjing, you can reach the conference location, Lake Home (No. 1528 Shuanglong Avenue, Jiangning District, Nanjing) either by subway or by taxi.

1. By subway

  • (1) Nanjing South Railway Station (By Subway line 3) — The ShengTai Road subway station — Lake Home.
  • (2) Nanjing Railway Station (By Subway line 3) — The ShengTai Road subway station— Lake Home.
  • (3) Nanjing Lukou International Airport (By Subway line S1) — Nanjing South Railway Station (By Subway line 3) — The ShengTai Road subway station— Lake Home.

2. By taxi

  • You can take a taxi directly to the conference location. This is the most convenient way to the destination. It may cost you about ¥18, ¥49 and ¥93 to reach Lake Home from Nanjing south railway station, Nanjing railway station and Nanjing Lukou international airport, respectively，which will correspondingly take you 8 min, 22 min and 35 min.

Tips

Currency

The official name for the currency of China is Renminbi (RMB). It is denominated into Yuan (¥). Foreign currency can be exchanged for RMB at the airport, banks and hotels. Major credit cards are honored at most hotels. Banks usually open at 9:00 in the morning and close at 17:00 in the afternoon all working days.

Climate and Clothing

Mid-June is both summer and the start of tourist season in Nanjing. The average temperature in Nanjing is about 18°C / 66.4°F - 31°C / 87.8°F. Generally, clothes such as long-sleeved blouses are enough for this time in Nanjing.

Electricity

Electricity is supplied at 220V, 50Hz AC throughout China.It's better to prepare the conversion switch.
